How to Play

Connect two F1 drivers through 6 or fewer mutual teammates.

Two drivers are considered teammates if they have raced in at least 1 Formula 1 Grand Prix for the same constructor. Reserve/test drivers and free practice rookie stand-ins don't count unless they actually participated in the Sunday race!

Example: Connect Lewis Hamilton to Max Verstappen
Lewis Hamilton
Jenson Button
Pierre Gasly
Sergio Pérez
Max Verstappen
Guess 1: Jenson Button
Correct - Lewis Hamilton and Jenson Button were teammates at McLaren from 2010-2012. Jenson and Max were never teammates, so the game continues.
Guess 2: Pierre Gasly
Incorrect - Jenson Button and Pierre Gasly were never teammates.
Guess 3: Sergio Pérez
Correct - Jenson Button and Sergio Pérez were teammates at McLaren in 2013. Sergio Pérez and Max Verstappen were/are teammates at Red Bull from 2021-2022, so the game is over.
